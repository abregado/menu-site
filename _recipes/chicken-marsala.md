name: Chicken Marsala
id: chicken-marsala
category: mains
pricePerServing: 6.99
prepTime: 35
difficultyRating: 4
calories: 450
ingredients:
  - 4 chicken breasts, pounded thin
  - 1 cup flour for dredging
  - 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
  - 1/2 cup Marsala wine
  - 1/2 cup chicken broth
  - 4 tablespoons butter
  - 2 tablespoons olive oil
  - Salt and pepper to taste
instructions:
  - Season chicken with salt and pepper and dredge in flour
  - Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat
  - Cook chicken until golden, about 5 minutes per side
  - Remove chicken and set aside
  - Add mushrooms to the pan and cook until browned
  - Add Marsala wine and chicken broth
  - Simmer until reduced by half
  - Add butter and return chicken to pan
  - Simmer for 5 minutes until sauce thickens
