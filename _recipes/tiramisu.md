name: Tiramisu
id: tiramisu
category: desserts
pricePerServing: 3.99
prepTime: 30
difficultyRating: 3
calories: 290
ingredients:
  - 6 egg yolks
  - 1 cup sugar
  - 1 pound mascarpone cheese
  - 1 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
  - 24 ladyfinger cookies
  - 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  - 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
instructions:
  - Whisk egg yolks and sugar until pale
  - Add mascarpone and vanilla, mix until smooth
  - Dip ladyfingers in coffee briefly
  - Layer dipped ladyfingers in serving dish
  - Spread mascarpone mixture over ladyfingers
  - Repeat layers
  - Dust with cocoa powder
  - Refrigerate for at least 4 hours
