name: Tofu and Vegetable skewers
id: tofu-skewers
category: grill
prepTime: 120
difficultyRating: 1
serves: 1
description: Vegan grill option
ingredients:
  - item: smoked-tofu
    amount: 350
    unit: g
    prep: cut into 24 slabs
  - item: capsicum-pepper
    amount: 1
    prep: cut into 12 slabs
  - item: mushrooms
    amount: 6
    prep: halved
  - item: zucchini
    amount: 1
    unit: g
    prep: sliced into 12 pieces
  - item: white-onion
    amount: 1.5
    prep: quartered then separated
  - item: sesame-oil
    amount: 2
    unit: tbsp
  - item: vinegar
    amount: 2
    unit: tbsp
  - item: maple-syrup
    amount: 2
    unit: tbsp
  - item: soy-sauce
    amount: 6
    unit: tbsp
  - item: garlic
    amount: 4
    unit: cloves
    prep: crushed
  - item: ginger
    amount: 0.5
    prep: fine grated  
instructions:
  - Prepare ingredients
  - Mix the liquid ingredients with the garlic and ginger to make the marinade.
  - Add one of each of the solid ingredients to each skewer. Add another tofu slab to finish it off.
  - Place the skewers into a tray
  - Paint the marinade over the skewers several times over the next 2 hours
